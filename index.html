About the Project

Remember when communicating with your friends online was easy? The simple interfaces, customizable to your heart's content and operating on an end-to-end principle where the only role of the app is to get what you want to say to whomever you want to say it to as fast and safely as possible. Straightforward, yet feature rich, this isolates your messaging from distractions and remains capable enough for everything from private messaging with old classmates to the soccer practice carpool. Easy messaging for everyday people.
